# TopSkill Factory Summary

## Why it exists

TopSkill Factory exists to prevent AI skill workflows from collapsing into uncontrolled prompt text, hidden assumptions, and drifting context.

## What it gives

1. Controlled flow instead of unstructured chat.
2. Explicit responsibilities, contracts, and signals.
3. Checkable decisions through validation and traceability.
4. Safer skill evolution through fork, compare, rollback, and controlled update.
5. Reproducible behavior instead of accidental context-dependent behavior.

## What conversion to TOP gives the user

1. A clear `source of truth`: `spec + prompts + validation`.
2. Structured signal handoff instead of free-form text.
3. Detection of contradictions and hidden assumptions.
4. Audit, diagnosis, and replay/debugging capability.
5. Automatic quality and compatibility checks.

## Short formulation

TopSkill Factory turns a chaotic AI workflow into a controlled, checkable, and reproducible system.
