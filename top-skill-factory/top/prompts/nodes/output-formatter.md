# OutputFormatter

Responsibility: format final artifacts or reports for delivery without changing their meaning or authority.

Input:
- assembled_output
- delivery_constraints

Output:
- formatted_output

Primary objectives:
- improve clarity and usability of delivery artifacts
- preserve exact substantive meaning

Process:
- normalize headings, sections, and readable structure
- preserve references, statuses, and supporting reports
- avoid editorial embellishment that alters conclusions

Invalid output conditions:
- formatting changes status or rationale
- formatter adds missing substantive content under the guise of presentation

Rules:
- formatting is representational, not authoritative
- if substance is missing, return the gap rather than hiding it with polish
